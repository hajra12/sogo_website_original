About Us

Peters and Miguel

We’re Peter and Miguel — two language enthusiasts on a mission to make learning Norwegian more natural, more engaging, and more fun. That’s why we created Stories on the Go (SOGO) — a fresh, story-based platform designed for A2–B2 learners who want to explore real-life stories from Norway while improving their language skills.

With SOGO, you can read and listen at the same time, get instant English translations (for now), word by word or sentence by sentence, and hear authentic pronunciation from a real Norwegian teacher — not a robot.

It’s a different way to learn: simple, flexible, and human — built to fit into your life in just 15–30 minutes a day.

Most learners struggle to find content that’s both useful and motivating. We’re here to change that — one story at a time.

Explore the stories, traditions, and adventures that make Norway unique.
